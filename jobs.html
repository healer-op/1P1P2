Customer Support Specialist

Help our Customers with Pre-Sales and Support Queries!

Overview

Modern Solutions is searching for a new addition to our team to assist with our growing need for community and customer support as the business continues to grow. We are looking for a candidate who has experience in either the VPS, web, or voice hosting space, or is familiar with our flagship RocketCAD product.

Modern Solutions was founded in October of 2017 with our first CAD product. In the last two years we have built a brand around our common goal of supporting gaming communities large and small in their efforts while maintaining superb customer support.

Learn More About Us

Responsibilities

Customer Support Specialists work as a team to find creative and exceptional solutions to every problem. Individually, Specialists will decipher and troubleshoot support queries as they come in to ensure a quick, yet superb response to our client’s questions.

Specialists work to evaluate each situation and determine the best course of action to remedy and introduce a solution. During support downtimes, Specialists work to introduce proactive solutions and prevent further issues from occurring.

Requirements
  • Be at least 17 years or older
  • Be a current resident of the United States
  • Have a basic familiarity with customer support systems (such as Discord, WHMCS, Crisp, Zendesk, Emailing, etc.)
  • Be incredibly patient and empathic
  • Have basic knowledge of VPS, web, and voice hosting
  • Have a basic knowledge of RocketCAD and the functionalities that it includes
  • Be able to communicate via Discord and Email with teammates
  • Able to work nights and weekends as scheduled
Bonus Items
  • Previous Customer Service experience (particularly in a remote setting and/or with a hosting company)
  • Fluency in Linux, SQL, Lua and/or C#
Reports To

Chief Technology Officer

Compensation

$10 hr, 20-25 hours/week
