Questions & AnswersFrequently Asked Questions

Is your question not listed?
Ask us here, or chat live now

1. Do you allow server query access?

We do! Submit a support request and our team will happily provide you with your server query information if you don't know it.

2. Can I change my Teamspeak location after I purchase?

Of course! You can transfer your Teamspeak to a new location at anytime by visiting your control panel.

3. Where is my Admin Priviledge Key?

Your privilege key was emailed to you in the Teamspeak welcome email. If you don't have that, you can also view it in your control panel or by reaching out to our support team.

4. Can I change my Teamspeak slots?

Yes! You can always upgrade/downgrade your Teamspeak slots by viewing your service in the Client Area and selecting "Upgrade/Downgrade".

5. I have a Teamspeak Snapshot from a previous server, can I use it on my Modern Solutions server?

Yes! We are always happy to upload snapshot files to your server. All you need to do is submit a support ticket through your Client Area.

6. Can I make a server backup?

We always believe that backing up files is a must. You can create a server backup at any time by logging into your voice control panel and selecting "Server Backups".
